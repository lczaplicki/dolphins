There are over 44 different species of dolphins. The species of dolphins that grows the largest would be the Orca Whales. They can grow to be about 25 feet in length and weigh up to 19,000 pounds. Dolphins can live very long lives. Depending on the species some can live up to 70 years! Dolphins are not scary. Most of them are actually very nice.
